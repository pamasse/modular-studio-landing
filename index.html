How It Works

From architect's plan to modular-ready in 3 steps

01

Import & Protect

Upload the architect's plan as-is (PDF, DWG/DXF, and soon IFC). Lock the elements that must stay fixed — permitted facades, stairwells, structural walls, technical shafts. Everything locked becomes an absolute constraint.

02

Modular Conversion

The plan is automatically split into buildable modules that respect your factory's constraints: transport dimensions, crane capacity, structural grid. Your catalogue modules are placed first to maximize repeatability. Residual spaces are filled with custom-fit elements.

03

Optimize & Export

Fine-tune the unit mix to maximize module repetition. Check zoning compliance. Export to Revit for execution. What used to take weeks now takes hours.

Features

Everything you need to go from plan to production

Six core capabilities designed for modular manufacturers who work with real architectural plans, not blank canvases.

Import existing plans

Upload the architect's plan as-is. PDF, DWG/DXF, and soon IFC. No need to redraw. The existing plan — with its permits, regulations, and design intent — is your starting point.

Freeze critical elements

Lock the elements that can't move: permitted facades, stairwells, technical shafts, load-bearing walls. Locked elements become absolute constraints. The architect stays in control.

Automatic modular split

The plan is split into modules that respect your factory's real constraints: transport limits, crane capacity, structural grid. Catalogue modules are placed first to maximize repetition.

Smart residual filling

Standard modules never cover 100% of a building. Corners, cores, and junctions are automatically filled with custom-fit elements that are dimensioned and buildable.

Unit mix optimization

Find the best combination of unit types (studio, 1-bed, 2-bed, 3-bed) to maximize module repetition, reduce production costs, and meet the program. Integrates exposure and orientation constraints.

BIM export to Revit

The modular plan goes straight into your existing BIM workflow. Every module and element is exported with its dimensions, type, and properties. The Revit model is ready for execution.
